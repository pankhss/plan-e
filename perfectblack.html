What is Date My closet ? 

The reason why we loved ‘Date My Closet’ and believe that it’s not just another rental website with big names is because, their team comprises of young millennials who look forward to style their customer with the best of fashion. 

They work in a very simple and 4 step way – Rent it, Size it, Flaunt it & Return it. And  this service is available across India, so no matter in which part of country you are. This website will work as a savior. However if you are from Delhi, things are more easy, as they offer at home sizing for you, since the company is based in Noida. Yay!
